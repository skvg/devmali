# Main Temple Complex Virtual Tour

Welcome to the heart of Devmali village's spiritual life - the Main Temple Complex dedicated to Lord Devnarayan. This sacred space has been the center of devotion and community life for over 1100 years.

## Architectural Marvel

The temple complex showcases traditional Rajasthani architecture with intricate stone carvings and beautiful geometric patterns. The structure has been carefully maintained while preserving its original design and spiritual ambiance.

### Key Features:

**Main Shrine (Garbhagriha)**
- Houses the sacred idol of Lord Devnarayan
- Adorned with traditional decorations and offerings
- Open for darshan during specific hours
- Maintained by hereditary priests

**Prayer Hall (Sabha Mandap)**
- Spacious area for community gatherings
- Hosts daily prayers and special ceremonies
- Features beautiful pillars with carved motifs
- Accommodates hundreds of devotees during festivals

**Courtyard (Pradakshina Path)**
- Circumambulation path around the main shrine
- Traditional stone flooring with drainage systems
- Surrounded by smaller shrines and meditation spaces
- Peaceful environment for contemplation

## Spiritual Significance

The temple serves as more than just a place of worship - it's the spiritual heart of the village where:

- Daily prayers and rituals are performed
- Community decisions are made
- Festivals and celebrations are organized
- Spiritual guidance is provided to devotees

## Visiting Guidelines

When visiting the temple complex, please observe these respectful practices:

1. Remove shoes before entering the temple premises
2. Dress modestly and appropriately
3. Maintain silence in the prayer areas
4. Follow the guidance of temple priests
5. Photography may be restricted in certain areas
